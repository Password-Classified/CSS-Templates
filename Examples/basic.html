Here is the full list of BASIC.CSS classes.

navbar A sticky navigation bar like on here
navbar-link A link within the navbar that glows on hover
logo A logo area with rounded corners that is darker than the main body. Use a div containing an image.
sec1 A simple, basic section with centered text and no borders
sec2
sec3 A darkened area around the text for emphasis or as a header/footer.
checkbox For a div with a label and an input of type="checkbox" that gives it a larger checkbox that glows on hover.
